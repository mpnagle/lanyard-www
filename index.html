Lanyard Studios is a software design and development firm, comprising of two principals, Michael Nagle and Larisa Berger. They have worked together since 2012 on user experience design, front-end web development and back-end web development. Current favorite tools include Sketch, Meteor, and node.js.

Past clients have included the New Jersey Center for Learning and Technology, Innovations Journal. Present clients include the San Jose Museum of Technology.
